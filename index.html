What students usually get

A teaching philosophy (in one card)

Hands-on + good judgment
Tools are necessary, not sufficient
We practice with real tools (SQL, Python, automation, analytics platforms), but always tie it back to controls, credibility, and decision-making.
Modern workflows
Efficient, explainable, repeatable
Emphasis on building workflows you can defend, document, and improve—especially when AI is involved.
Career-ready communication
Explain the “why”
Students practice translating analysis into clear insights for technical and non-technical audiences.
